Magical Hat (a concise guide on how to create one)

How to craft the Magical Hat

On this website page i will just show you all the steps and necessary ingredients to make the perfect magical hat for all your magical needs

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of cloth
  • a pinch of rainbow dye
  • a whole lot of fun
  • 3 drops of magic juice

Steps to create your Magical Hat

  1. Prepare the cloth
  2. Open your rainbow dye box and take a small amount to put on the cloth
  3. Have a whole lot of fun
  4. Pour 3 drops of the magic juice on the cloth
  5. Enjoy your colorful hat
