THE IMPOSSIBLE RUBY

How to craft the impossible ruby (it might kill you during the crafting)

Ingredients

  • a random stone from a random trail
  • a strong belief
  • a hammer

The process

  1. Place your stone on a table
  2. Concentrate allyour belief into he exact middle part of the stone
  3. Hit the stone with your belief infused hammer, just one hard hit
  4. The stone will now expand in a red aura and quickly become of an impossible shape
  5. If you survived the previous step, you should now be the proud owner of the impossible ruby
